Welcome to SparkER's documentation!
===================================
Quick introduction
------------------

Welcome to SparkER library's documentation. 
SparkER is an open-source python library developed under 
Apache Spark by UNIMORE professors and engineers to enhance Spark functions to
work effectively and efficiently with the long lasting problems of entity resolution and data deduplication.

The source is available on GitHub.

Feel free to contact us to give us tips for new features or troubleshooting.
